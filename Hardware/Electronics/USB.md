- USB 3.0 renamed as USB 3.1 Gen 1 (5 Gb/s)
- USB 3.1 renamed as USB 3.1 Gen 2 (10 Gb/s)

## USB Type-C

Can transport (but not always supported):

- data
	- USB 2.0
	- USB 3.0 (3.1 Gen 1)
	- USB 3.1 (3.1 Gen 2)
- audio & Video
	- DisplayPort (video)
	- HDMI (video)
	- audio (USB Audio)
- power
	- charge standard
	- USB Power Delivery fast charge

Power: 10 Watts to 100 Watts (differents profiles)
